Click The Box

What is Click The Box?

Click The Box is a very entertaining clicker game created by the Algebra Together team. Your goal is to get as many click as you can in the shortest period of time. To make this game more exciting and more entertaining, we have added many unique features such as shape changing, color changing, extra time, extra points and many more. You can get also get levels and achievements in is game so you can find a goal to work towards to. In the near future, multiplayer will be added which will allows players to battle each other and try to get to the #1 on the leader-board. Let's don't waste your time and start playing Click The Box today!
